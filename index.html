Why Napa?

Napa, a wine lover's paradise

Napa is best known for its world-class wineries and exceptional Cabernet Sauvignon. With hundreds of vineyards to explore, you'll find everything from cozy, family-run tasting rooms to grand estates with sweeping views of the valley.

The food scene is just as impressive, offering everything from casual eateries to Michelin-starred restaurants. There is no shortage of delicious meals to enjoy alongside a perfectly poured glass of wine.

I visited in 2024 and completely fell in love with Napa. If you're a wine enthusiast, it's the perfect place to uncork, relax, and enjoy the best of what wine country has to offer.

WINERIES

My favorite wineries

Chappellet

Chappellet is a family-owned winery on Pritchard Hill, offering stunning views of Napa Valley and Lake Hennessey. It's best known for its exceptional wines and for establishing Pritchard Hill as a premier winemaking region.

Address:

1581 Sage Canyon Rd,
St Helena, CA 94574

Phone:

(707) 286-4219

What I like about it

This hilltop winery offers a cozy, intimate atmosphere and stunning views. The wines are beautifully balanced and luxurious, with standouts like the Signature Cabernet, Hideaway Cabernet, and a crisp, bright Chenin Blanc that’s truly one of a kind.

Piazza Del Dotto

Piazza Del Dotto is one of Napa’s most well-known wineries, offering immersive cave tours with generous pours, a wide variety of wines, lush Italian-style gardens, and delicious bites from its in-house kitchen.

Address:

7466 St. Helena Hwy
Napa, CA 94558

Phone:

(707) 963-2134

What I like about it

Piazza Del Dotto offers one of Napa's most memorable tasting experiences, with a fun and informative wine cave tour, generous barrel samples, and delicious food from their in-house kitchen. The beautifully landscaped grounds and Italian-inspired décor make it as stunning as it is satisfying.

Learn more

Paraduxx

Paraduxx is a Napa Valley winery, known for its bold, artful red blends. With a sleek tasting room and seated experiences, it offers generous pours in a relaxed, modern setting.

Address:

7257 Silverado Trail
Napa, CA 94558

Phone:

(707) 945-0890

What I like about it

Paraduxx focuses on blends, setting it apart in a region known primarily for Cabernet. They offer a fun, interactive experience where you can create your own red blend, along with a variety of indoor and outdoor tastings and more approachable price points—making it a great option for wine lovers on a budget.
